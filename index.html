Help / Glossary
EV
Expected value. Positive EV means your taken odds are better than the reference/fair price.
Min. Odds
The minimum playable odds implied by the valuebet calculation. If your taken odds are higher than this, the bet stays +EV.
CLV
Closing line value. Compares your taken price to the later/manual closing price.
Tracked vs Hidden
Tracked means you took the bet. Hidden means you cannot or do not want to bet it, so future scans suppress it.
Detected markets
Exact market names learned from raw API responses. Tick them when normal market groups reject a wanted bet.
Auto-scan
Works while this page/app stays open. A backend is required for reliable background alerts.
